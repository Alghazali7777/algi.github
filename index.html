HISTORY OF COFFEE

"Coffee became known to the Gallic tribes in East Africa in 1000 BC. Entering 5 AD, coffee was already known in remote parts of Ethiopia. Then in 700-1000 AD coffee was first known by the Arabs as an energy drink (for staying up late). The spread of coffee began at the same time as the spread of Islam. The first source of coffee was in Mocha, an area in Yemen."
